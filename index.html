Ruby Day!

Ruby Unconference


Nov. 12, 2011   |   Neumont University   |   South Jordan, Utah   |   9 AM - 5 PM

One day, one track

Like the best bike trails in Utah, Ruby Day is single-track. This puts a lot of enthusiastic members of Utah's Ruby community all sharing their knowledge and passion in one open space. It also allows for lots of flexibility in the schedule.

What you make of it

Unconferences aren't bound by many rules. Attendees show up, decide what they hope to get from the day, and help set the agenda. People from all skill levels are welcome. Be prepared to suggest topics or even to present on them!

Fork anytime

Like most unconferences, we live by "the law of two feet": if you aren't getting (or contributing) what you hoped, no one's feelings will be hurt if you decide to "fork" the agenda by breaking out and starting your own session at any time.
